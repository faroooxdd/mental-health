If you need to talk to someone anonymously, search for "Sourire de Reda" (mostly for youth) or use Dabadoc to find therapists who offer video calls.

Immediate Panic Fix

Match your breath to the circle. Slow down.

The "Sour" Trick: Bite into a lemon or something super sour. It shocks your nervous system out of a spiral.

Actually Helpful Advice

  • Get off your phone: Scrolling is poison when you're spiraling. Put it in another room for 20 minutes.
  • Change your temp: Take a freezing cold shower or a very hot one. It resets your brain's "emergency" signal.
  • The "Brain Dump": Write down every single thing bothering you on a piece of paper. Then rip it up. It sounds cliché, but it offloads the mental weight.
  • Don't: Don't make any permanent decisions today. Your brain is lying to you right now because it's tired.
  • Don't: Avoid caffeine or sugar right now; they mimic the physical symptoms of anxiety (shaking, heart racing).

Reality Check

You’ve survived 100% of your bad days so far. This is just a wave. It feels like a wall, but it’s a wave. Let it wash over you, don't fight it, and it will recede.

Stay safe. You matter.
